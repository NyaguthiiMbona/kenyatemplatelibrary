Financial Statement

Preview of a simple financial statement format commonly used in Kenya.

Revenue Streams Amount (KES)
Sales Revenue 150,000
Service Revenue 50,000
Total Revenue 200,000
 
Expenses Amount (KES)
Salaries 50,000
Rent 20,000
Utilities 10,000
Marketing 15,000
Total Expenses 95,000
 
Net Income 105,000

Tip: In Excel, calculate totals using formulas like =SUM(B2:B3) and net income as =Total Revenue - Total Expenses.
