Attendance Sheet

Preview of a monthly employee attendance register commonly used in Kenya.

Employee Name Department 1-Dec 2-Dec 3-Dec ... 31-Dec Total Present Total Absent
John Doe Sales P P A ... P 20 2
Jane Wanjiku HR P P P ... A 22 1

Legend

P = Present
A = Absent
L = Leave

Tip: In Excel, calculate totals using formulas such as =COUNTIF(C2:AG2,"P") for Total Present and =COUNTIF(C2:AG2,"A") for Total Absent.
