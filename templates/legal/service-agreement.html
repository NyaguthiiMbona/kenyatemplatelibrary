What this agreement covers

Use this template to define scope of services, payment terms, duration, confidentiality and termination for client engagements.

Main clauses

  • Scope of services and deliverables
  • Fees, invoicing and payment terms
  • Duration and termination
  • Confidentiality and intellectual property
  • Liability and governing law

How to use this template

  1. Download the DOCX file.
  2. Complete provider and client details.
  3. Define deliverables, timelines and payment schedule.
  4. Sign and keep a copy for both parties.

FAQ

  • Can I require deposits? Yes. Include payment terms and milestones in the Fees section.
  • Is a service agreement enough for large projects? For major projects get legal advice and consider schedules or annexes.
