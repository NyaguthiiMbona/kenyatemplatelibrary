What this agreement covers

Use this template to define capital contributions, profit sharing, management roles, decision making and dissolution procedures for a partnership.

Main clauses

  • Names and contributions of partners
  • Profit and loss sharing ratios
  • Management and decision procedures
  • Dissolution and dispute resolution

How to use this template

  1. Download the DOCX file.
  2. Enter partner names, capital contributions and profit sharing percentages.
  3. Agree and sign with witnesses present.

FAQ

  • Do partners need to register the partnership? Yes. Partners should register the business and comply with tax requirements.
  • Can the profit split be changed? Yes. Amend the agreement and sign by all partners.
