What this NDA covers

This Non-Disclosure Agreement protects confidential information shared between parties. Use it to make sure business, technical and financial details remain private.

Key sections in this document

  • Parties and contact details
  • Definition of confidential information
  • Obligations of the receiving party
  • Term and termination
  • Governing law (Republic of Kenya)

How to use this template

  1. Download the DOCX file.
  2. Replace bracketed fields with your details.
  3. Review confidentiality period and obligations.
  4. Both parties sign and keep a copy.

FAQ

  • Is this legally binding in Kenya? It provides standard contractual protection. For high-stakes deals consult a lawyer.
  • Can I change the terms? Yes. Edit the document in Word or Google Docs.
