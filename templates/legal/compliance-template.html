What this checklist covers

This checklist helps small businesses track registration, tax, HR, financial and industry-specific compliance tasks.

Checklist items

  • Business registration and KRA PIN
  • County permits and industry licenses
  • PAYE and VAT registration where applicable
  • Employment contracts and workplace policies
  • Annual financial statements and reconciliations

How to use this checklist

  1. Download the DOCX file.
  2. Complete items relevant to your business and track status.
  3. Keep records and mark completed items for audits.
